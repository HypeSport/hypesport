Calindagan Triumphs in Thrilling Finale, Secures Kagawad Boni Catarata Memorial Cup

by Elijah De Jesus

October 25, 2023

Calindagan Basketball team

In a thrilling showdown on Thursday night, the Calindagan basketball team emerged victorious in the 2023 Kagawad Boni Catarata Memorial Cup Inter-Barangay Sports Competition, securing the championship title with a nail-biting final score of 67-64 against Daro. The fervent supporters of the Calindagan squad erupted into a boisterous celebration as their team clinched the coveted title, marking a triumphant culmination to a fiercely contested basketball season. The championship game showcased exceptional skill, determination, and sportsmanship from both teams, creating an electrifying atmosphere in the packed venue. The driving force behind Calindagan's success was none other than Aljun Poblacion, who earned the prestigious title of the tournament's Most Valuable Player (MVP). Poblacion's stellar performance on the court undoubtedly played a pivotal role in securing the championship for Calindagan. Despite a valiant effort, Team Barangay Daro had to settle for the Runner-up position, reflecting the intense competition that unfolded on the basketball court. The Daro squad showcased resilience and skill throughout the tournament, making them formidable opponents in every match. Meanwhile, Barangay Camanjac emerged as the 2nd Runner-up after a hard-fought battle that resulted in their victory over Poblacion 1 (Tinago) with a final score of 79-72 in the pre-championship game. This victory solidified Camanjac's position as a top-performing team in the competition. The awarding ceremony, led by Councilor Maria Marife Cordova and Sangguniang Kabataan Renz Macion, added a touch of prestige to the conclusion of the matches. The ceremony honored the outstanding players, recognizing their contributions to the thrilling sports spectacle that captivated the community. As the cheers echoed through the venue and the champions reveled in their hard-earned victory, the 2023 Kagawad Boni Catarata Memorial Cup will be remembered as a showcase of athleticism, teamwork, and the indomitable spirit of the Calindagan basketball team.
